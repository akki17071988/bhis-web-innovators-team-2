Key Factors Contributing to Hunger:

  • Poverty: One of the most significant drivers of hunger is poverty. Poor households lack the income to purchase food, access land for cultivation, or invest in proper nutrition.
  • Conflict and Displacement: Armed conflicts and political instability displace millions of people, disrupting food production and access to markets.
  • Climate Change: Changing weather patterns, extreme events like droughts and floods, and environmental degradation harm food production and exacerbate hunger in vulnerable regions.
  • Food Waste: Roughly one-third of the food produced for human consumption is lost or wasted. Reducing food waste is critical to improving food security worldwide.
